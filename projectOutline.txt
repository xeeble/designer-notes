This webpage should display design ideas incorporating text and images.
Each design idea is its own "article".

Users should be able to:
- "flip" through the pages of the designers notebook and see the designs.
- Bookmark/Favourite designs to return to them easily.
- Leave comments on design ideas.
- Say/Vote if they have used the idea in their own work.
- Contribute their own design ideas to the notebook*

*Ambitious goal, not part of MVP
